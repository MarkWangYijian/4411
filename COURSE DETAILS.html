Course recommendation

Business Administration

With our Certificate in Business Administration you’ll learn the advanced level administrative skills to manage and supervise others in any type of organisation: business, government, private and nonprofit. Want to lead with confidence? This course can help you advance your career and prepare you for managerial and top-tier administrative roles. Get equipped with the skills and knowledge to manage key information in any organisation.

Website development

This course contains a combination of coding and management units and is ideal to help you take your first steps into entering the website development industry, teaching you to create and manage basic websites from concept to launch. It also provides the requisites to undertake the Diploma that will take your learning to the next level with advanced concepts that allow you to create fully customised websites and hosting solutions.

Diploma of Information Technology

Learn knowledge and skills to design, analyse, implement and manage the cyber security strategies, frameworks, management processes and technical cyber security controls that minimise cyber security vulnerabilities and risks to business operations and practices. Our classes, courses and assessment are all created with you in mind; with experienced instructors, hands-on training that consider your unique situation.

Undergraduate Certificate in History and Philosophy of Complementary Medicine

The Undergraduate Certificate in History and Philosophy of Complementary Medicine is a perfect qualification to introduce you to the historical underpinnings and cultural background of some of the oldest health fields. The subjects have been selected to provide a broad insight across many complementary therapies in Australia. Students will study the history of healing and integration of complementary medicine, naturopathic principles and philosophy, foundations of Chinese medicine, musculoskeletal therapies as well as the cultural, ethical and social dimensions of human nutrition.

Veterinary Assistant & Welfare Certificate

The value-packed course will teach you all about the most important aspects of caring for animals, preparing you to work alongside veterinarians or even further your journey to becoming one. Upon completion, you’ll be fully prepared to help provide both routine and emergency animal care and feel confident knowing you have the ultimate edge in this increasingly competitive industry. If you want to make a genuine difference in the lives of animals great and small, this course will give you the hands-on skills to get straight to work.

Data Analytics Fundamentals

This online short course will provide you with the fundamental skills you need to launch your career in data analytics or apply data-thinking to your current role. During this six week course, you’ll learn valuable skills in data analysis that you can translate into many roles and contexts. Add analytical capabilities to your existing skillset, learn the fundamentals of SQL and Tableau, and discover how to manipulate, analyse, and communicate data insights using spreadsheets. Learn to produce evidence-based insights from data, how to detect patterns, create visualisations and build dashboards.
